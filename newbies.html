Hi! Welcome to Neopets Classic!
So, this is just a quick run down of some things that are good to know before you jump in. If you have any other questions there is a discord link on the Dailies page, feel free to come find us in the questions channel.


Timers

•Shop Restock - every 7 minutes, restating at :00
•Random Events - reset after 8 minutes
•Employment Agency - every 10 minutes, restarting at :00
•Healing Springs - Every 30 Minutes
•Wheel of Excitement - Every 2 Hours
•Scratchcard Kiosk - Every 4 Hours

Money

•Restocking
•Random Events
•Employment Agency
•Scratchcard Kiosk

New Features

•Pet Tokens! - these let you add more than 4 pets to your account!
•Inventory Tokens - Increase inventory space
•Name Tokens - Change Pet/Account names

Known Bugs

•Places on maps do not work - These areas are not yet active
•Getting 500 error pages when trying to access site - This means the server is DOWN. If you want to double-check, you can go here and check the server's status: https://downforeveryoneorjustme.com/neopetsclassic.com
•Fruit Machine - Sometimes 3 matches will not award a prize. Fix pending.
•Morphing Potions - When you use a morphing potion it WILL SAY that your pet morphed into whatever it was previously. The potion will work fine though so try to remain calm <3

Anything Else?

Jellyclassic has tons of resources to help you get started. If you have any other questions please head over to the Discord and ask. We're all so excited to have new people coming in
