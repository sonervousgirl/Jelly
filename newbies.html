Newbie Guide
By Mousekat                                                                   4/5/2021


Hi! Welcome to Neopets Classic!
So, this is just a quick run down of some things that are good to know before you jump in. If you have any other questions there is a discord link on the Dailies page, feel free to come find us in the questions channel.

You can also visit the Jellyclassic FAQ for more detailed answers and more questions!


Timers

•Shop Restock - every 7 minutes, restating at :00
•Random Events - reset after 8 minutes
•Employment Agency - every 10 minutes, restarting at :00
•Healing Springs - Every 30 Minutes
•Wheel of Excitement - Every 2 Hours
•Scratchcard Kiosk - Every 4 Hours

Money

•Restocking
•Random Events
•Employment Agency
•Scratchcard Kiosk (gamble)
•Wheel of Excitement (gamble)

Lingo

•RS - Restock(ing)
•RE - Random Event
•Rain - Lots of good REs
•NPC - Neopets Classic
•Retail - Regular Neopets

Random Events

•REs come in waves
•Lab Map and codestones will drop the same type in chunks
•Boochi will usually either always hit or always miss when he's active
•If you see him active be prepared by using a Boochi Shield
•Shields are: pets that are already baby, you want baby, can't turn baby, or have an inexpensive paint job

Account Related

•Edit User info by going to help > User Info
•Account limit is %, 1 main, 4 sides
•You CANNOT use sides to gain NPS
•Healing Springs and Omelette can be used on sides ONLY to feed pets until the lodge is done
•You CAN freely transfer items from your Main to sides
•Only large RE wins can be transferred from your sides back to your main
•Noah has extensive logs and will be able to see if you're doing this more than a normal amount

The Pound

•The pound is FULL of great names
•Center button and search are broken
•Quick Adopt Link: https://neopetsclassic.com/adopt_pet/?pet_name=
•QAL Generator - Link

New Features

•Pet Tokens! - these let you add more than 4 pets to your account!
•Inventory Tokens - Increase inventory space
•Name Tokens - Change Pet/Account names

Known Bugs

•Some places on maps do not work - These areas are not yet active
•Search is broken, to look up pets/names go to an existing pet and use the URL to search, this is CaSe SeNsItIvE
•Getting 500 error pages when trying to access site - This means the server is DOWN. If you want to double-check, you can go here and check the server's status: https://downforeveryoneorjustme.com/neopetsclassic.com
•Fruit Machine - Sometimes 3 matches will not award a prize. Fix pending
•Morphing Potions - When you use a morphing potion it WILL SAY that your pet morphed into whatever it was previously. The potion will work fine though so try to remain calm <3

Anything Else?

Jellyclassic has tons of resources to help you get started. If you have any other questions please head over to the Discord and ask. We're all so excited to have new people coming in
